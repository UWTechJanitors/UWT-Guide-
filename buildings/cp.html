About

Address

Info

Actually three buildings connected together, Cherry Parkes was original warehouses for merchants supplying the Alaskan Gold Rush. Now it houses classrooms and faculty offices. It is the home of three academic programs:

➤ School of Engineering & Technology

➤ School of Nursing & Healthcare

➤ Institute for Innovation & Global Engagement (home of the Global Honors program)
