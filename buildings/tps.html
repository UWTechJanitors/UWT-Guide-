Info

Tacoma Paper & Stationery includes classrooms, computer and engineering labs, the Urban Design Lab and the Global Innovation & Design Lab.

We also honor the campus's biggest supporters in a display on the main floor made from old wood beams recovered in the renovation of the building in 2016.

The main floor was home to the Old Spaghetti Factory in Tacoma for over 40 years and some say you can still smell the pasta sauce on a hot day.
