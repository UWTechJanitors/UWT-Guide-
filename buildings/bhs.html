Info

Like the Birmingham Block, Birmingham Hay & Seed is primarily a set of classrooms. But it has a secret. If you walk the narrow hallway that leads around behind BHS 106, you'll find a small two-story atrium lounge looking over Pacific Avenue that makes a quiet place to study or hang out because so few people know it's there.
