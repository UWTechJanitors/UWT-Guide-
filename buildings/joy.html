Info

Do you like taking classes? Then the Joy Building will live up to its name. Named for its original developer in 1912, Russell T. Joy, and not the euphoric feeling learning may give you, the building is home to more classrooms than any other UW Tacoma building.

Chances are good, you will have at least one class in the Joy Building. We hope you graduate feeling some joy.
