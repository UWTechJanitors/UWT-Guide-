Reserving Study Rooms

How to Reserve Study Rooms

➤ If this is your first time reserving study rooms, please refer to the tutorial video below and use the steps as a reference. Otherwise, tap on the Reserve Space button to reserve a room.

Step 1

➤ Tap on the Reserve Space button. Once you arrive at the page, scroll all the way down and click on the link within the purple box labled "Complete the required online tutorial." After clicking the link, log in using your UW student ID and password.

➤ Tap Course Catalogue to complete the online training using the 25Live reservation system.

Step 2

➤ Refresh your web browser to enable express scheduling. Check your UW email for a direct link to the 25Live website.

Step 3

➤ Select a study space from the Express Locations dropdown menu. Select a date and time to reserve this study space. Create an event name. Then, press the save button.

Step 4

➤ You will receive confirmation that the study space has been successfully reserved on the 25Live page and in your UW email.
