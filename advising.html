Academic Advising Appointments

Newly admitted and current UW Tacoma students can schedule an appointment online with their assigned pre-major academic advisor.

➤ Appointments must be scheduled at least one day (24 hours) in advance.

➤ During peak periods (e.g. registration), we highly encourage scheduling two weeks in advance.
